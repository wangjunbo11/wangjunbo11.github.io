## 🔬 Research Experience

### Agricultural Export Duration and Market Entry (SCI Q1)
Published in *Journal of the Science of Food and Agriculture* (First author)

This study investigates how agricultural export duration affects firms’ entry into new markets using HS6-level trade data from China. The results show a nonlinear (inverted U-shaped) effect, mediated by information search costs and total factor productivity.

### Digital Technology and Green Total Factor Productivity
National Undergraduate Innovation Project (Principal Investigator)

This project examines the impact of digitalization on agricultural green total factor productivity using provincial panel data (2015–2022). We construct a digitalization index using the entropy method and measure green productivity using the NDDF-MML approach. Empirical results are based on a two-way fixed effects model and mediation analysis.

### Genetic Algorithm for Multi-objective Optimization (IEEE Conference Paper)
First author

This study develops a genetic algorithm-based optimization framework for production decision-making, balancing detection efficiency, cost minimization, and loss probability under uncertainty.
