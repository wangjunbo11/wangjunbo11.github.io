## 👋 Hi, I'm Junbo Wang

I am an undergraduate student at China Agricultural University, majoring in Finance, with a strong interdisciplinary background in Computer Science and empirical economics.

## 🌍 Research Identity

My research focuses on **climate change, agri-food systems, and environmental-economic transitions**, with an emphasis on how **trade dynamics, ESG practices, and digital transformation jointly shape agricultural productivity, carbon emissions, and sustainable development**.

I primarily use **causal inference and econometric methods** (DID, panel data models, IV, and synthetic control) combined with large-scale trade, environmental, and firm-level datasets.
